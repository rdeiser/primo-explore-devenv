How does this work?

Search It provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

Search It also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create favorites lists
  • Export citations in a variety of formats
  • View full search results (Some databases, only show results when you're signed in.)
